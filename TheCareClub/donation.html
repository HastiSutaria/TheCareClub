Why donate to The Care Club ?

Because we are an organization started and led by business and technology leaders who are volunteering their time. Your donation goes to delivering food, medication and other essential items into the hands of individuals and families who need it most. They are the 300 million+ people in our country who are homebound, who are time starved and most importantly, who need our commitment, our generosity, our helping hands to survive.
